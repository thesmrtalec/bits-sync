The four stages

every bit climbs this ladder

concept

There's something funny here. You can feel it. You can't prove it yet.

bit

Find the funny and put it in words. No perfection here — we can keep editing!

ready

Ready to say out loud. To strangers. On purpose. Yikes.

proven

It got laughs. In public. Validation at last, you needy little freak.

capture

Write it down. You won’t remember it in 30 seconds. WRITE IT DOWN. The concept / bit toggle says which drawer we toss it in.

tags

Casually organize your mess. Buzzwords of your choice — topic, voice, theme, intended audience. Tap one to filter; the ones you use most rise to the top.

workbench

Tap a bit to open its workbench — the line up top, a scratch pad below for rewrites and angles. Each bit keeps its own.

notes

Endless blank space to fuel your existential crisis. Exercises, brain-dumps, half-thoughts — the stuff that isn’t a bit.

vault

Dead bits in your basement. Think there’s still meat on the bone? Take a look. Ready to say goodbye? Delete for good from here.
