The pipeline

stuff in, sets out

stuff

Confessions, observations, half-thoughts. Anything true enough to write down. Lines never leave.

bit

Find the funny and put it in words. Bits get timed, reacted, and strung into sets.

set

String bits in order against a clock. This is the thing you say on stage.

capture

Write it down. You won’t remember it in 30 seconds. WRITE IT DOWN. The tab you’re on says which drawer it lands in — stuff or bits.

tags

Casually organize your mess. Buzzwords of your choice — topic, voice, theme, intended audience. Tap one to filter; the ones you use most rise to the top.

the gym

Tap a bit to take it into the gym — the line up top, the speed bag for jabs, a scratch pad below. Each bit keeps its own.

notes

Endless blank space to fuel your existential crisis. Exercises, brain-dumps, half-thoughts — the stuff that isn’t a bit.

vault

Dead bits in your basement. Think there’s still meat on the bone? Take a look. Ready to say goodbye? Delete for good from here.
